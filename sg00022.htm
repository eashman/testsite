Services Offered


    Dr. Ashman offers quality surgical & anesthesia care covering the full scope of the specialty of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery. Some of the services available in our practice include:

    *Dental Extractions including Removal of Impacted Wisdom Teeth.

    *The Office Fully Staffed & Equipped to provide Ambulatory Sedation & Nitrous Oxide Analgesia and General Anesthesia.

    *Dental Implant Surgery.

    *Full Scope of Soft Tissue & Hard Tissue Facial Trauma Surgery.

    *Treatment of Oral Pathology including Cysts & Tumors of the Jaws.

    *Oral Cancer Examinations, Biopsy and Treatment

    *Biopsy and Diagnostic Services

    *Correction of Dentofacial Deformities (Orthognathic surgery).

    *Jaw Reconstruction following Facial Trauma or Cancer Surgery.

    *Bone and Soft Tissue Graft Surgery

    *Pediatric Oral Surgery including Orthodontic Extractions and Surgery

    *Preparation of the Mouth for Dentures

    The staff will be happy to assist the patients in processing their insurance forms.
