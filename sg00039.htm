Diagnostic Services

    Often, patients will notice a sign or symptom within their mouth or in the head and neck region for which they can not identify a cause. Frequently, a patient's physician or dentist will observe an abnormality in a patient's mouth or face which they are not able to diagnose. In situations such as these, Dr. Ashman utilizing the patient's history and physical examination, special radiographic studies, laboratory studies, surgical exploratory procedures and biopsies can help diagnose these problems and recommend therapies or refer the patient to the appropriate specialist.
